Welcome to Biships Place

Bishops Place, a boutique apartment community conveniently located in desirable West Hartford, brings life together with an abundance of amenities and incomparable modern style. Our one-of-a-kind community features uniquely designed studio, one-, and two-bedroom apartment homes. No detail has been missed as each unit boasts a spacious open floor plan, large Andersen® windows that let in an abundance of natural light, and a gourmet kitchen featuring stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, and a quartz-stone island. Start your day in our state-of-the-art fitness center with yoga room or with a hot cup of coffee or tea in our Bishops Café. Barbecue with your friends, gather around the fireplace in our beautifully landscaped courtyard or take in the stars from our rooftop deck. When you feel like stepping out, the shops and restaurants of vibrant West Hartford Center and Blue Back Square are nearby as is every imaginable convenience that Bishops Corner has to offer including Starbucks® and Whole Foods®. Convenient living. Modern Style. Bishops Place.
